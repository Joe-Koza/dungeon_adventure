DUNGEON ADVENTURE

You are a knight on a quest, seeking treasure and adventure. You have heard many stories about this ancient dungeon. It is said that the dungeon was created by an eccentric noble as a test for his heirs, to see if they were "worthy" of inheriting his fortune. Apparently, none of them were...

Many adventurers after them entered the dungeon, but none of them returned. You hope you do not meet the same fate. Standing at the entrance, you take a deep breath and march forward into the darkness.

After a while, the path splits into two hallways. The left one is lit by torches, illuminating the skeletons of fallen warriors. The right hallway is dark, and empty. Suspiciously empty...
